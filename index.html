Ultimate Clarity: 4K Dash Cam Recording

True 4K Front & 2K Rear Dual Channel Recording

Genuine 3840x2160P Ultra HD front and 2560x1440P (2K) QHD rear synchronized recording. This dual dash cam system ensures comprehensive coverage with high image fidelity, precisely capturing details like license plates.

Sony STARVIS 2 Sensor for Superior Night Vision

Equipped with Sony's latest STARVIS 2 image sensor (IMX678) for exceptional low-light performance. Captures bright, clean, detailed footage even in very dark conditions, making it your reliable night vision dash cam.

Advanced HDR (High Dynamic Range) for All Conditions

HDR technology for both front and rear cameras masters challenging lighting, balancing detail in bright highlights and deep shadows, ensuring your HDR dash cam consistently delivers clear, balanced video.

Wide-Angle Field of View for Full Road Coverage

Captures a broad road area, including multiple lanes, crucial for recording the full context of accidents or unexpected events. The rear camera also offers a wide view for comprehensive monitoring.

Intelligent & Convenient: WiFi & Voice Control

Intuitive Voice Control System

Hands-free operation for core functions like starting/stopping recording, taking photos, and locking videos via simple voice commands. This voice-activated dash cam significantly reduces driver distraction.

High-Speed 5GHz Wi-Fi & Dedicated App

Built-in dual-band (2.4GHz/5GHz) Wi-Fi for seamless connection with a dedicated app (iOS & Android compatible). Wirelessly preview, download, share videos, and adjust settings. The 5GHz band offers faster, more stable transfer speeds for your WiFi dash cam.

2.0-inch IPS High-Definition Screen

Clear display for instant live feeds, playback, and settings adjustment. IPS technology ensures excellent color reproduction and wide viewing angles on your car camera screen.

Efficient H.265 Video Encoding for 4K Files

H.265 (HEVC) compression offers up to 50% smaller files than H.264 while maintaining 4K video quality, allowing for longer recording times and faster video transfers.

Ultimate Protection: GPS, Parking Mode & Supercapacitor

24/7 Parking Surveillance with Included ACC Hardwire Kit

Supports round-the-clock parking monitoring with an included ACC hardwire kit. This parking mode dash cam intelligently switches to low-power mode, recording time-lapses or G-Sensor triggered events for hit-and-run incidents.

Built-in High-Precision GPS Module

This GPS dash cam records trajectory, real-time speed, time, and coordinates, embedding this data into video files for verifiable context in insurance or legal claims.

High-Sensitivity G-Sensor for Impact Detection

The G-Sensor dash cam feature detects sudden movements (collisions, impacts), triggers emergency recording, and automatically locks key video segments to prevent crucial accident footage from being overwritten.

Supercapacitor Power for Enhanced Durability & Safety

Uses a supercapacitor, far more resilient to extreme temperatures (-20°C to 70°C / -4°F to 158°F) than traditional lithium batteries. This ensures reliable dash cam performance in both intense heat and freezing cold, offers a longer lifespan, and eliminates risks associated with lithium batteries, such as swelling or fire, making the S9 Max a safer and more durable choice.

Includes a 6.5-meter (approximately 21.3 feet) rear camera extension cable, solving installation challenges for larger vehicles like SUVs, MPVs, minivans, and pickups.
